Our mission

Clotherizer is an initiative by us high school graduates to raise awareness about the LGBTQ community. We aim to discourage fast fashion and use the sales from our website to donate to charity. We hope to bring a positive change with our initiative and inspire others to do so too. This website hopes to bring a new fashion that allows our users to be more creative with their choices - there is no combination that does not look good.
